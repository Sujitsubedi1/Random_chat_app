👶 Child Safety Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2025

At Random Chat, protecting children is a top priority. We have strict safety guidelines in place to ensure the platform is not misused.

🚫 Not for Children Under 18

This app is strictly intended for users 18 years or older. By using this app, you confirm that you meet this age requirement. If we become aware that a user is under 18, their access may be revoked.

🧠 No Discussion Involving Minors

  • No content involving minors
  • No asking about or impersonating minors
  • No inappropriate or suggestive conversations targeting underage individuals

Violators may be permanently banned and reported to appropriate authorities.

🛡️ Moderation & Reporting

We automatically flag suspicious content using keyword detection and user reports. Reported chats may be reviewed and users violating safety rules may be removed silently.
